Frequently Asked Questions

What is SkillVault?

SkillVault is an online learning platform that offers a wide range of courses across various categories to help you acquire new skills, advance your career, and achieve personal growth.

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply create an account, browse through our course catalog, and click on the course you want to enroll in. Afterward, follow the on-screen instructions to complete your enrollment.

While some courses on SkillVault are free, many others require a subscription or one-time payment. We offer a variety of pricing options to suit your needs and goals.
